    About Me

    I am a final year Master's student in Mathematics (Pathways in Statistics).
    I am interested in Mathematical Biology, Computational Biology, Applied Mathematics. Recently, I am also exploring Statistical Modelling and Bayesian Statistics.
    I am excited to apply mathematical models and techniques to better understand the complexities of biological systems. Nowadays, I am fascinated to study models in ecology.
